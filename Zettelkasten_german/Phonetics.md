## letters
`s` sounds like `З` or `z` - `Sie`
`z` sounds like `ц`
`ß` sounds like `ss`
`ö`sounds like `ё`
`v` sounds like `ф` или `f`
`ä` sounds like  `э`




letter `h` in a syllable doesn't have a sound, but makes the previous vowel long
`Der Lehrer` учитель
## combinations
`ie` sounds like `ии` - `Sie`
`ei` sounds like `ай`
`eu` sounds like `ой`

`ch` sounds like `хь`
`sp` sounds like `шп`
`sch` sounds like `щ` `[ʃ]`
`tsch` sounds like `тч(щ)` `[tʃ]`

 `ch` nach(after) `a, o, u, au` = `[x]` → Buch
 `ch` nach(after) `e, i, ä, ö, ü, l, n, r` = `[ç]` → ich, Milch
