Ich **bin** - i **am**
du **bist** - you **are**
er/sie/es **ist** - he/she/it **is**

wir **sind** - we **are**
ihr **seid** - you (plural) **are**
sie **sind** - they **are**
Sie **sind** - YOU **are**

| German    |          | English     |      |
| --------- | -------- | ----------- | ---- |
| pronouns  | verb     | pronouns    | verb |
| Ich       | **bin**  | I           | am   |
| du        | **bist** | you         | are  |
| er/sie/es | **ist**  | he/she/is   | is   |
| wir       | **sind** | we          | are  |
| ihr       | **seid** | you(plural) | are  |
| sie       | **sind** | they        | are  |
| Sie       | **sind** | You         | are  |
